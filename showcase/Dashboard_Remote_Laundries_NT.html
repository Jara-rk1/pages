Scabies infestation leads to secondary skin infections, Group A Streptococcus, rheumatic fever, rheumatic heart disease, post-streptococcal glomerulonephritis, and sepsis. Each progression multiplies healthcare costs by 10-100x.

Each stage of disease progression multiplies healthcare costs by 10-100x. Laundry access intervenes at the earliest and most cost-effective point in the cascade.
